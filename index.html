History- Durham House of Pets was founded in 1909 by Samuel Wilkins. Samuel Wilkins grew up on a farm in Burlington, Vermont. He moved to Durham, New Hampshire permanently after studying agriculture at the University of New Hampshire. When Wilkins opened the store he sold food for livestock. Subsequently, the store was formerly known as Durham House of Farming. The store primarily serviced the university; although it did serve some local farms. In 1940, Samuelâ€™s descendant Maryann Mahoney transformed Durham House of Farming into a pet store; and changed the name to Durham House of Pets. The store carried dogs, cats, rabbits, fish, reptiles, pet food and livestock food. The store also now serviced the greater Durham area; alongside the University.

Why Use Us- Durham House of Pets has only carried top of the line products ever since our grand opening in 1909. We only sell products of the highest quality because that is what we believe our customers deserve. In addition, our store has been serving the local population for decades and we take pride in our relationship with the community. Accordingly, our store sponsors the annual Durham Summer 5k. This 5k helps raise money for after school programs for local Durham children. Durham House of Pets is the best pet store around, because we are focused on selling quality products and supporting our community.
